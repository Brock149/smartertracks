Delete Your SmarterTracks Account

We respect your right to remove your personal information from our systems at any time. You have two ways to permanently delete your SmarterTracks account and associated data:

  1. Via Your Supervisor — Any supervisor at your company can delete your account from within the SmarterTracks Web Admin Portal. Please contact your supervisor and request removal.
  2. Email Request — Send an email to brockcoburn@smartertracks.com from the email address associated with your account. We will verify your identity and permanently delete your data within 30 business days.

Account deletion is irreversible. All data linked to your user profile will be removed from our databases and cannot be recovered. Please export or back up any information you might need before requesting deletion.

If you have any questions, contact us at brockcoburn@smartertracks.com.
